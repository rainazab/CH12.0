Product Description:
====================

OPTION 1 (Recommended - Most Detailed):

Rho is a SaaS platform that helps developers discover, compare, and integrate APIs. We offer a subscription service with three tiers: Free Plan (1 API comparison per day with basic features), Pro Plan ($10/month for unlimited comparisons, advanced features, and priority support), and Enterprise Plan (custom pricing for teams with dedicated support). Users pay monthly subscriptions through Stripe for Pro and Enterprise access. The platform aggregates data on AI APIs (GPT-4, Claude, DALL-E, Midjourney, ElevenLabs, etc.) and displays cost, latency, uptime, and performance metrics to help developers make informed technology choices.


OPTION 2 (Simple & Professional):

Rho is an API comparison platform that helps developers choose the best APIs for 
their projects. We offer monthly subscription plans ($10/month for Pro, custom pricing 
for Enterprise) that give users unlimited API comparisons, advanced analytics, and 
priority support. The free tier allows 1 comparison per day.


OPTION 3 (Very Concise):

SaaS platform providing API comparison and recommendation services through 
subscription pricing. Free tier: 1 comparison/day. Pro: $10/month unlimited.
